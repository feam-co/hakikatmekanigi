Design Principles

What we believe.

01

Deterministic Over Probabilistic

Governance decisions must never depend on LLM outputs. Rules are explicit, auditable, and reproducible.

02

Humans Govern, AI Advises

AI proposes actions. A deterministic rule engine evaluates. Humans retain final authority.

03

Cryptographic Truth

Every decision is sealed in an immutable chain. If it happened, we can prove it.

04

Fail-Fast, Not Fail-Silent

Violations trigger immediate rejections. The system stops before damage occurs.
