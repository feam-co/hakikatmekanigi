Why FEAM.co

Compliance by architecture,
not by assertion.

🚧 Deterministic Over Probabilistic

Governance decisions must never depend on LLM outputs. Our rule engine is explicit, auditable, and reproducible. Every decision follows the same path, every time.

🔐 Cryptographic Truth

Every decision, override, and rejection is sealed in a SHA-256 linked Witness Chain. If it happened, we can prove it. Tamper-evident by design.

⚖️ Humans Govern, AI Advises

AI proposes actions. A deterministic rule engine evaluates. Humans retain final authority through structured oversight — exactly as EU AI Act Article 14 requires.

🚫 Fail-Fast, Not Fail-Silent

Constraint violations trigger immediate HTTP 422 rejections. The system stops before damage occurs. No silent mutations, no unwitnessed overrides.
